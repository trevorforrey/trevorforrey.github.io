Darkstalkers Themed Stick (2019)

Since my wife and I started dating, we've playing fighting games together. For the longest time she played on an old ps2 stick with an adapter and one Christmas I decided to make a better stick for her. This stick was made based off her favorite series, Darkstalkers, with a focus on her favorite character, Morrigan.

Research

Before deciding on how and what to build, I first searched a Darkstalkers Art Book we have for inspiration on colors and for artwork to use on the fight stick.

Design / Etching

After finding some images that I really liked, I started out processing them for a cover and back panel etching. I was lucky enough to go to a college that has a laser cutter and etcher. Which made manufacturing this stick significantly easier. I decided to set it up to cut on my first pass, that way I avoided any burn scars on the final etching on the back of the front panel.

Box Assembly

Once all the pieces were etched I was able to sand, glue, assemble, and finish the wood part of the fight stick.

Final Piece

I picked out some parts to go along with the artwork. For the stick hardware I used: Hori Hayabusa (joystick), Sanwa OBSF 24/30mm (buttons), and a pre-installed Brook Board.

Street Fighter Themed Stick (2016)

The first fighting game I got into was Street Fighter: Third Strike. I got hooked playing one summer with my wife while we were dating. While watching tournaments I had seen cool custom fight sticks and decided to give it a go in creating my own.

Research

I started out by finding all the resources available for building a custom fight stick. For creating the stick artwork I went through a bunch of different online Street Fighter comics to get inspiration for the stick and create an understanding of the type of color palette I wanted to use.

Box Assembly and Design

Building this stick was my introduction into a lot of techniques. In the process on building it I learned how to use a laser cutter, how to solder, and the basics of wood making. I also experimented with some different stick artwork.

Final Piece

Throughout this project I was able to learn a lot about prototyping tools. It was also nice being able to do some graphic design for the collage on the face of the stick. When I made this I was pretty crazy about Street Fighter and I'm glad I was able to learn something and be productive through my excitement of the series.
